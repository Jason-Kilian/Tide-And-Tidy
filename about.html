Discover the Beauty of the Southern Coast

Welcome to Struisbaai

Nestled along South Africa’s breathtaking southern coastline, Struisbaai offers a unique blend of relaxed seaside charm and natural beauty. Known for its long white sandy beaches and peaceful atmosphere, it’s the perfect destination for those seeking rest, connection, and a slower pace of life.

Just minutes away lies Cape Agulhas, the southernmost tip of Africa, where the Indian and Atlantic Oceans meet. This iconic landmark offers dramatic ocean views, historic charm, and unforgettable sunsets that draw visitors from around the world.

Whether you're enjoying a morning walk along the shoreline, exploring the local harbour, or taking in the fresh ocean air, this region promises a memorable coastal experience unlike any other.
